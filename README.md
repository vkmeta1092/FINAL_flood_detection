# 🌊 Urban Flooding & Hydrology Engine (INDIA INNOVATES 2026)
### *“Know Before it Pours: A Proactive Shield for Delhi’s Heartbeat”*

Urban flooding in Delhi isn't just a data point; it’s a city-wide standstill that traps families, stalls ambulances, and paralyzes our economy. This engine is a **Situational Intelligence Platform** designed to move Delhi from reactive chaos to predictive certainty.

## 🚀 The Human Impact (Key Features)
- **Hybrid Safety Grid:** We’ve mapped **2,500 high-risk zones**—balancing the painful lessons of 1,250 historical flood sites with 1,250 predictive simulations to protect neighborhoods before they become headlines.
- **The "Perfect Storm" Simulator:** An interactive stress-test engine that calculates the combined impact of monsoon rainfall, Yamuna backflow, and—most importantly—the invisible "choke factor" of clogged drains (Silt/Trash).
- **A Digital Lifeline:** Our automated **2-Way Alert System** skips the red tape. It instantly dispatches the **Top 3 Hotspots (via DigiPIN)** to the nearest NDRF units via Telegram and Email, ensuring help arrives before the gridlock starts.
- **Visual Command:** One-click integration with **Kepler.gl** transforms complex hydraulic math into a 3D story, allowing planners to see which "structural straws" are under too much pressure.

## 🛠️ The Engine Room (Technical Stack)
- **Core:** Python Flask (The brain of the simulation).
- **Geospatial Intelligence:** Folium, GeoPandas, and Shapely (Mapping the 100km radius of Bharat Mandapam).
- **Precision Logistics:** Haversine Algorithm for dynamic emergency unit assignment.



## 🔮 The Road Ahead: A Living Immune System
We are evolving this simulator into a real-time **Hydrological Immune System** for the city:
- **Advanced Sight:** Integrating **SAR Imagery** to see through cloud cover and **Ensemble Averaging** (XGBoost/Random Forest) for military-grade predictive accuracy.
- **Live Pulse Injection:** Moving beyond static records to ingest real-time feeds from **IMD/CWC sensors** and **AQI.com data** (correlating air debris with drainage blockages).
